How to Recycle & Upcycle Electronics

Recycling means turning old electronics into new things. Upcycling means making something old into something new and cool!

Recycling Steps

  1. Find a recycling center for electronics.
  2. Remove batteries and personal data.
  3. Drop off at a special e-waste collection point.
  4. Watch it get turned into new gadgets!

Upcycling Ideas

Turn old phones into planters, or old keyboards into art!
