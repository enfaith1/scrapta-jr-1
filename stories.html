Fun Stories & Facts About E-Waste

Read exciting stories and learn cool facts to make learning about e-waste fun!

The Adventure of Little Gadget

Once upon a time, there was a little gadget named Sparky. Sparky was an old phone who felt sad because he was no longer used. But one day, his friend took him to a recycling center. There, Sparky was turned into a shiny new toy! The end.

Cool Facts

  • Every year, people throw away enough e-waste to fill a football stadium!
  • Recycling e-waste can recover valuable metals like gold and silver.
  • Upcycling an old computer can make it into a cool planter for your room!
